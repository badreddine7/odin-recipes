Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe is quick and easy, perfect for a weeknight dinner. It features a rich tomato sauce and al dente pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Toss spaghetti with sauce. Top with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
