Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a favorite for many. Layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella make it a perfect comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until well browned. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. Simmer for about 30 minutes.

  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Arrange noodles over meat sauce. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese mixture, then top with mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
