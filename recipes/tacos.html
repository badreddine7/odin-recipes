Tacos

Description

These tacos are a crowd-pleaser, featuring seasoned beef, fresh toppings, and warm tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa for topping

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain fat.
  2. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions.
  4. Fill shells with beef mixture and top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
